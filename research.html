The phytoplankton communities in space & time

An integrative approach to study salt lakes of Kazakhstanian arid and semiarid zones. Research assistantship on a contract under the supervision of Dr. Natalie Barteneva. Used imaging flow cytometry - FlowCam (Yokagawa Fluid Imaging Technologies), classified phytoplankton taxa, performed DNA isolation and PCR, Next generation sequencing on the MinION (Oxford Nanopore Technologies). Abstract accepted to SIL 2022, Berlin: Novel view on a framework of Microcystis spp. blooms: imaging flow cytometry analysis of bloom development in mesocosm experiment. Co-authored a poster accepted by ISAC 2022 Congress in Philadelphia, USA: Imaging Flow Cytometry Analysis Of Climate Change Scenarios In Freshwater Aquatic Systems: A Case Of Microcystis spp. Blooms.
